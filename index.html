Three ways to work together

Most engagements start with a focused diagnostic. From there, we go as deep as the situation calls for.

01
Entry point · 1–2 weeks
Product Readiness Check

A focused diagnostic engagement. We map your current product situation — team alignment, strategic clarity, roadmap health, and execution risks — before you commit budget or begin building. Concrete, actionable, and fast.

02
Core engagement · 4–12 weeks
Product Strategy & Roadmap Design

User research done for you, synthesised into clear product priorities. A co-created strategy and sequenced roadmap — what to build, when, and why — aligned with your business goals and your organisation's actual capacity to execute.
